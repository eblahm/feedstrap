The Weekly Reads Report is prepared by the Strategic Studies Group (SSG) in the Office of Policy.  It is a biweekly report meant to provide situational awareness on the issues SSG is analyzing, monitoring and discussing.  The report contains links to all the articles along with some preliminary analysis. Although it is an informal product pertaining to current research efforts, an aggregate database of the articles will be used as one tool in the formal Environmental Scan process.
